Four languages. Fifteen minutes. One demo. 🌍

ElevenLabs handled the voice, Claude handled the translation, and I didn't record, translate, or re-edit a single word. I wrote the narration once, in English.

Last post, Claude drove Playwright through a live app and ffmpeg stitched the steps into a narrated MP4. The two gaps I wanted to close were the robotic voice and no localization. ElevenLabs (https://elevenlabs.io/) gives me a British narrator, "George", that lands close to a real person, and its multilingual model voices the same script in French, Spanish and Japanese. One source script in, four videos out. 🎧

This is AI translation, and I don't speak those languages, so I can't verify the output myself. Anything customer-facing still wants a native-speaker review.

Cost? Nothing. It fit inside ElevenLabs' free tier (10,000 credits a month) and used my existing Claude subscription.

The demo is live here: https://levantar-ai.github.io/demos/claude-elevenlabs-video-walkthrough/
